Services at a marketplace generally facilitate transactions and support both sellers and buyers, encompassing a variety of functions to ensure a smooth, secure, and engaging experience for everyone involved. This includes providing the platform for vendors to showcase their goods, managing payment processing and logistics to help buyers receive their products efficiently, and offering marketing and branding tools to boost the visibility of local vendors. The marketplace may also offer customer support, dispute resolution, and community-building events, fostering trust and loyalty among its users and enhancing the overall shopping environment.
